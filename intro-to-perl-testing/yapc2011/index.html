YAPC::NA 2011 Presentation

I gave a presentation on the Introduction to Perl track on Testing at the YAPC::NA conference for 2011. The slides are available as an S5 presentation, which basically means they can be viewed in any standards-compliant browser.

If you print the slides, the result will contain notes on each slide that covers what I planned to say as well as what was displayed. You can see these notes in the browser by clicking on the slashed 0 symbol that appears when you mouse over the lower right portion of the page.
